About KindieSpirits

Indie game studio. Small team. Big passion.

// In-game screenshots — Veneer V∅lt

We make games about
things going wrong.
Because every failure is just a system reset.

KindieSpirits is an independent studio born in 2026 from a personal challenge. After leaving his own gaming company in Italy, our founder decided to start over on his own terms—to prove he could build something right, his way. What started as a one-man mission grew into something much more serious. His sister joined to handle the art, turning a solo struggle into a family journey. That’s where the name comes from: Kin (family) and Indie. We are Italians building our future in Germany. We believe that even in these crazy years, there is still a place for those who aren't afraid to reset and try again.
